AngularJS ng repeat directive
	ng-repeat directive in Angular
	Nesting ng-repeat with an example
	Take indexes of ng-repeat

	ng-repeat directive is similar with foreach loop in other languages
	To find the index of an item in the collection use $index property
	To get the index of the parent element there are two ways:
		Use $parent.$index or
		Use ng-init="parentIndex=$index"
